Welcome To The Frogger Arcade



You start out with 5 lives. Every time you make it to the water you get 10 points and then you are reset. If you get a star you are invincible for 30 seconds. If you get a heart then your lives are increased by one. Keys are worth 20 points, orange gems are worth 30, green gems are worth 40, and blue gems are worth 50 pts, (Because they are the color of my eyes!).

Select a Character to start the game
